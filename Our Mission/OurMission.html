Our Mission

Drinking safely is our priority

A 2015 study out of San Francisco estimated that one-third of sexual assault cases involved a "date rape drug". These substances are unknowingly slipped into drinks, leaving the victims vulnerable to theft, kidnapping and drug-facilitated sexual assault (DFSA). With an alarming number of cases each year, we, at GlassIntact, firmly believe that it is about time something is done about this.

We believe you should be able to drink safely anywhere you go. Through our products, we hope to save more people from consuming spiked drinks. Your priority is to have fun while we ensure your drinks are 100% safe.
